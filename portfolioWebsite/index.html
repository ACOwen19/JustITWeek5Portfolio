About Me

Hi, I'm Archie,

I decided to move into development after working for a Telematics software provider as a Technical Support Analyst. During my time there I worked closely with the development team which led to a burgeoning interest in programming. To start my career as a professional developer I completed several online developer courses before joining the DPP provided by JUST IT, an intensive and rigorous self-funded course which helped me to grow improve my skills in C#, ASP.NET, MVC, SQL & JavaScript/HTML/CSS.

Outside of work I enjoy playing rugby, competitive card games, cookery and reading fantasy & sci-fi. I'm keen to take my next step into the world of development.
